Professional Profile: Data Governance and Analytics Leader, PMP certified and actively pursuing CDMP. Expertise in improving governance maturity from Level 1.2 to 3.4 in one year. Specializing in translating data strategies into measurable outcomes (cost avoidance, risk mitigation).

Introduction & Interest

I am highly motivated by IO’s mission to modernize public infrastructure and its clear strategic direction to centralize governance under Strategy and Insights. IO is navigating a pivot toward becoming a data-centric enterprise, managing over $30 billion in active projects.

Strategic Alignment: IO's decision signals a commitment to closing the recognized "data maturity gap" [103, Preamble]. My experience delivering over $173K in annual automation savings aligns perfectly with IO's mandate to establish a foundation for AI and Digital Twins.

The Framework: Hub-and-Spoke

My approach is a Federated Governance Model (Data Mesh principles), balancing standardization with agility.

  • The Hub (Strategy & Insights): Sets the "Constitution"—mandatory standards, security protocols, and interoperability.
  • The Spokes (Business Units): Maintain Domain Ownership (e.g., Major Projects) and implement governance locally.

The Six Core Pillars

1. Structure & Roles

RACI Model: Data Owners (Accountable), Stewards (SMEs), Custodians (Tech).

5. Seal of Trust

Formal badge (Bronze to Platinum) classifying data as trusted and audit-ready.

Role-based training programs and DAMA Maturity Assessments.
